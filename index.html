Welcome to Sea Sharks

We provide Onshore and Offshore Commercial Diving and Marine Services.

The company started at the beginning with ship chandler services. Company’s activities spreaded since then in all marine services & works with the following activities.
Ship chandlers to all kinds of ship passing the Suez Canal, The Mediterranean sea and Red Sea area including provision, deck, Engine materials, transit stores .
Also, Our Company provide under water services including bottom inspections by photos and video, welding, cutting, cleaning and polishing propellers.
All Kind of marine survey including Consulting, general examinations insurance, draft and condition survey etc.
